Spirituality & Wellbeing

Being spiritual well can play an important part on you wellbeing, maintaining a good mental health and recovering from mental health problems. Bellow you can find some apps that can help you on your path to a healthy mind and a healthy life;

  • Calm
  • Stop, Breathe & Think
  • Headspace
  • MindFi

Another thing you can do to improve your mental health based on the spiritual aspect of your life is to be part of your local community, trough volunteering or joining a support group.
