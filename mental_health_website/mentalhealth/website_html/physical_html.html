Physical

Physical activity is very beneficial for your mental health (and wellbeing) , but most of the time it is viewed as something we “have to do” or “should do” instead of being something beneficial and positive to our wellbeing. See the bellow image for the benefits of physical exercise for your mental health;
Being active is does not mean gong to the gym or doing a high intensity type of sport, there are unconventional ways of being active like, taking a long walk in a park or dancing, for example. Everyone is different and what works for some/most people might not work for you.

Bellow you can find a quiz, that based on your likes and dislikes will guide you to the most suitable exercise for you.

  • I want to have some fun

The most suitable exercises for you would be yoga, skipping or even dancing, these workouts are very easy to follow through a tutorial and can easily be done at home or you can book a class at your near gym.

What we would suggest for you is a sport that you can play with others like basketball, dodgeball, if you are feeling a little bit more adventurous, why not invite your friends to your local park for a run or even a hike?!

The ideal type of sport for you would be something to get your blood flowing, our suggestions are maybe a CrossFit class, a cardio session at your local gym or even a session of HIIT (high-intensity interval training).
